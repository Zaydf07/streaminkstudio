Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

StreamInk Studio ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

1. Information We Collect

DataWhy we collect it
Email addressAccount creation, login, transactional emails
Password (hashed)Authentication — we never store plaintext passwords
Content you generateTo save and sync your documents across devices
Credit usageTo enforce plan limits and show your usage
IP address / request logsSecurity, fraud prevention, rate limiting
OAuth tokens (if connected)To publish content to social platforms on your behalf

We do not collect payment card data directly — payments are processed by Stripe.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Service
  • To enforce credit limits and billing
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, password resets)
  • To investigate abuse or security incidents
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your content to train AI models.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on AWS infrastructure in the US. Document content is stored encrypted at rest. OAuth tokens are stored server-side with time-to-live expiry.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Groq — AI text generation. Your prompts are sent to Groq's API. See Groq's privacy policy.
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery.
  • Supabase — Database and authentication.
  • Railway — Application hosting.

5. Cookies

We use only essential cookies — a session token to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Download all your data from Settings → Data & Storage
  • Delete — Delete your account and all data from Settings → Danger Zone
  • Correct — Update your email or profile at any time in Settings
  • Portability — Export your documents as HTML, PDF, or DOCX from each editor

7. Data Retention

If you delete your account, your personal data and documents are deleted within 30 days. Anonymised usage statistics may be retained for analytics.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We'll notify you by email or in-app notice for material changes.

10. Contact

Privacy questions? Email privacy@streamink.io.
