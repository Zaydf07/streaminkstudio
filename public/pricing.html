Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about StreamInk Studio pricing.

What is a credit and how many do I need? +
Credits are the currency for AI actions on StreamInk. A blog post costs 15 credits, an article 10, a social post 3, an email newsletter 8, a PDF export 5, and an AI Refine suggestion 2. Free users get 100 credits a month — enough for about 6–7 blog posts or 30+ social posts. Pro users get 2,000 and Agency users have no limit.
What happens when I run out of free credits? +
Once your 100 free monthly credits are used up, new AI generations and credit-spending actions will pause until the 1st of the next month — when your credits reset automatically. You can upgrade to Pro or Agency at any time to continue immediately with a higher allowance.
Do unused credits roll over? +
On the Free plan, unused credits expire at the end of each month. Pro and Agency users get rollover: up to one month's worth of unused credits carry forward (up to 2,000 on Pro; unlimited on Agency), so a quiet month means extra headroom next month.
Can I try Pro before paying? +
Yes — start on the Free plan and explore all six engines with 100 credits. You can upgrade to Pro at any time. We offer a 14-day refund guarantee on all paid plans, no questions asked.
What happens to my documents if I downgrade? +
Your documents are never deleted. If you downgrade from Pro to Free, your existing documents remain accessible — you just won't be able to create new ones beyond the 10-document limit, and your monthly credits drop back to 100.
Can I change plans at any time? +
Absolutely. Upgrade any time — the new credit allowance kicks in immediately and you're charged the prorated difference. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
